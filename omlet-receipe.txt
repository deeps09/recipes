Ingredients:
 - 2 Eggs
 - 1 tsp edible oid
 - salt
 - green chilly
 - sabji masala
 - slice bread

Directions:
  1. In a medium saucepan, pour some oil and let it get warm.
  2. Break eggs from knift or spoon and pour yolk and albumin in a cup and mix it with chilly, salt powder and sabji masala.
  3. Pour egg mixture on saucepan and put two bread slice on top of eggs layer.
  4. Let it be cooked and flip it over to cook it from other side and serve it on a plate with some corriender leaves.
